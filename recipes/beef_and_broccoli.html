Beef & Broccoli

Indulge in the savoury goodness of our Beef and Broccoli recipe! Savour tender slices of beef stir-fried to perfection, paired with vibrant broccoli florets bathed in a flavorful soy-based sauce. This quick and easy-to-follow recipe promises a symphony of tastes and textures, making it a delightful weeknight dinner that will satisfy your cravings. This recipe serves 4 people.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of thin strips of steak
  • 3 minced cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tsp of grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 80ml of soy sauce
  • 50g of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 4 cups of broccoli floret
  • 2 tbsp of cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp of water
  • sesame seeds (as desired)
