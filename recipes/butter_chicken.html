Butter Chicken

Experience the rich and aromatic flavors of our Butter Chicken recipe. Succulent pieces of chicken bathed in a velvety tomato and butter sauce, infused with a medley of spices, create a symphony of taste that's both comforting and indulgent. This quick and authentic recipe guarantees a restaurant-quality dish right in the comfort of your own kitchen. Elevate your culinary journey with our delightful Butter Chicken – a culinary delight that's as easy to make as it is to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of oil
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 medium diced onion
  • 1 tsp of ginger paste
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 and 1/2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into chunks)
  • 4 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp of garam masala
  • 1 tsp of chilli powder
  • 1 tsp of fenugreek
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 tsp of black pepper
  • 1 cup of yogurt

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet or medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the oil, butter, and onions and cook onions down until lightly golden, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add ginger and garlic and let cook for 30 seconds, stirring so it doesn’t burn.
  4. Add the chicken, tomato paste, and spices.
  5. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until everything is cooked through.
  6. Add the yogurt and let it simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve over Basmati rice or with naan.
  8. Enjoy!
